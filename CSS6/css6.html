FAQs
How does URL shortening work?

URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, condensed version that redirects to the original URL. When a user clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the intended destination.

Is it necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service?

No, it is not necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service. You can shorten URLs without signing up, making it quick and easy to share links. However, creating an account offers additional features such as tracking your shortened links and managing them effectively.

Are the shortened links permanent? Will they expire?

The shortened links are generally permanent as long as the URL shortening service remains operational. However, some services may have policies that could lead to expiration if the link is not accessed for a certain period. It's always a good idea to check the terms of service of the specific URL shortening provider you are using.

Are there any limitations on the number of URLs I can shorten?

No, there are no limitations on the number of URLs you can shorten. You can shorten as many links as you need, making it easy to manage and share your content.

Can I customize the shortened URLs to reflect my brand or content?

Yes, you are allowed to customize the shortened URLs to better reflect your brand or content.

Can I track the performance of my shortened URLs?

Yes, we will provide analytics that allow you to track the performance of your shortened URLs..

How secure is the URL shortening service? Are the shortened links protected against spam or malicious activity?

Our URL shortening service employs advanced security measures, including encryption and regular monitoring, to protect against spam and malicious activities. We prioritize user safety and ensure that all shortened links are scanned for potential threats before being generated.

What is a QR code and what can it do?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of matrix barcode that can be scanned using a smartphone camera. It can store various types of information, such as URLs, text, or contact details, making it easy for users to access content quickly by scanning the code. With our URL shortener, you can generate QR codes for your shortened links, allowing for seamless sharing and easy access to your content.

Is there an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into my own applications or websites?

No, we do not offer API services at the moment. However as our compan expands, we aim to add in that functionality as well.
